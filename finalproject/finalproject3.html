5. Christmas Lights

While Kanye West's hit song "All of the Lights" captures the vibrant energy of various illuminations, it sadly overlooks the enchanting magic of Christmas lights. These radiant decorations covering the neighbourhood during the holidays bring a warm, colourful ambiance and whimsy that deserves its moment in the spotlight. Truly one of the best Christmas things.
